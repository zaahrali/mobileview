About Me

Taking pictures is a form of self expression. I enjoy taking pictures of beaches, particularly beaches in Florida (where I'm from). I especially enjoy the various colors you see, at different times, on the exact same spot! Some of the pictures I share are of the same place but look so different.



  • These are some other photographers that do beach photography:

  • Massimo Vitali is an Italian photographer best known for his large-scale color images of beaches.

  • Amber Mozo is an acclaimed surf photographer and the daughter of legendary surf photographer Jon Mozo.

  • Gray Malin is a Los Angeles-based fine-art photographer who does aerial beach photography.
